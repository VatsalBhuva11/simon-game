This game tests how well can you remember a pattern. Start off the game by pressing any key on the keyboard. Then, a tile gets lit. Pressing the correctly lit tile will progress you further into the game. With each consecutive level, an ADDITIONAL tile gets lit.

Your goal is to remember the sequence of tiles lit up, and for each level, CLICK THE TILES IN THAT SEQUENCE, plus the additional lit up for that level.
